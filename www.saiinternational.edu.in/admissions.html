Frequently Asked Questions

SAI International School offers two curriculum options:

  1. CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) – Builds strong academic foundations and prepares students for national and international competitive exams.
  2. Cambridge International (IGCSE, AS and A Levels) – Encourages creativity, critical thinking, and global perspectives, ideal for students aiming for higher education abroad.

To apply for admission:

  1. Visit the Admissions Office to collect an Enquiry Form, or fill the Admission Form online at https://saiinternational.edu.in/admission/
  2. Contact the Admissions Counsellors at (0674) 7100200, 9338169966, or 9338336699.
  3. Connect via WhatsApp at <strong> 9338169966 or 9338336699.</strong>
  4. You can also email admissions@saiinternational.edu.in for any queries.

The age criteria for each grade are defined as per the school’s admission policy and board guidelines. Please refer to the official age eligibility table available at the time of admission.

For Nursery to Grade III, there is no written test. A personal interaction is conducted with the Academic Coordinator and the Principal. For Grade IV and above, students are required to appear for a written assessment followed by a personal interaction.

List of required documents:

  1. Two recent passport-size photographs of the student, father, and mother
  2. Birth certificate issued by a municipal or government authority
  3. Aadhaar card or valid ID proof of student and parents
  4. Transfer Certificate (original) from the previous school
  5. Copies of the last two years’ report cards
  6. Proof of residence such as electricity bill, rent agreement, Aadhaar, passport, or voter ID
  7. All documents must be authentic and up to date. Originals may be required for verification.
