Ethos of SAI

The ethos of SAI International School is the holistic development of the body, mind and soul of the children. To achieve the highest level of health, well-being, and purpose in life, the school follows the basic principle of focusing on creating a balance in the six main areas that make up a person’s life: Physical, Social, Emotional, Spiritual, Intellectual, and Purposeful.

Read More about our Ethos

Guru Shishya Parampara

SAI International has incorporated the timeless values of the Guru Shishya Parampara. Our deep rooted belief is enshrined in the fact that a Guru is believed to be the one who indoctrinates the Shishya to attain the ultimate truth or satya and mokshya – the salvation.

Our

Founder

Late

Dr. Bijaya

Kumar Sahoo

“Education is not just the filling of a mind, but the shaping of a soul. At SAI, we don't just teach we transform.”

Dr. Bijaya Kumar Sahoo believed that every child carries within them a limitless light — and that a school’s true duty is to help that light shine.

A pioneer in 21st-century education, he founded SAI International School with a dream to blend global excellence with Indian values. His vision gave rise to one of India’s finest schools — where curiosity is celebrated, creativity is nurtured, and character is shaped with care.
